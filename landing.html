How it works

From registration to service in 4 steps

The system guides you through a simple process so you never have to wait in line again.

01

Create your account

Register with your national ID, phone number, and age. Your profile determines priority eligibility.

02

Choose a service

Select from 7 categories and 30+ sub-services. Pick a date and an available time slot.

03

Receive confirmation

Get a booking confirmation with your queue number sent to your phone before your visit.

04

Arrive and be served

Show up at your scheduled time. Your number is called and you receive service immediately.
